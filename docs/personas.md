# Shadow — Personas

## 1. Primary Persona: Overloaded Builder

### Profile
The Overloaded Builder is a founder, product manager, developer, creator, consultant, or independent specialist who constantly manages many active threads at once.

They usually have:
- several projects in progress
- many unfinished tasks
- career ambitions
- creative ideas
- personal goals
- financial pressure
- scattered notes
- inconsistent routines
- emotional overload
- long-term plans without one clear system

### Core problem
They have too much context in their head and no simple way to externalize, structure, and review it.

They do not want another complex productivity system. They want a system that understands messy input and turns it into structure.

### Typical thoughts
- “I have too much in my head.”
- “I know I’m forgetting something important.”
- “I need clarity, not another app to maintain.”
- “I want to see what is actually happening in my life.”
- “I need one place for tasks, thoughts, goals, and reflections.”

### Jobs To Be Done
When I feel overloaded by tasks, thoughts, and plans, I want to quickly dump everything into one place so that I can reduce mental pressure and regain clarity.

When I write messy notes about my day, I want the system to understand and organize them so that I do not have to maintain a complex structure manually.

When I review my week, I want to see patterns and neglected areas so that I can make better decisions.

### Needs
- fast capture
- AI auto-organization
- daily focus
- task extraction
- goal tracking
- memory retrieval
- weekly pattern analysis
- low-friction interface

### Shadow value
Shadow becomes their external thinking layer: a place where chaos becomes structured life intelligence.

## 2. Secondary Persona: Reflective Creator

### Profile
The Reflective Creator is an artist, musician, writer, designer, content creator, or creative person with an intense inner life.

They generate many ideas, emotions, drafts, references, concepts, and identity questions.

### Core problem
They feel a lot, think a lot, create a lot, but often lose structure and consistency.

They may have:
- creative bursts
- unfinished concepts
- emotional swings
- lack of routine
- unclear priorities
- scattered notes
- tension between inspiration and discipline

### Typical thoughts
- “I have many ideas, but I lose them.”
- “My emotions affect my output.”
- “I want to understand my patterns.”
- “I need a system that respects creativity, not just productivity.”
- “I want to connect my inner state with my creative work.”

### Jobs To Be Done
When I have a creative idea or emotional insight, I want to capture it instantly so that it does not disappear.

When I feel blocked, I want to see what patterns usually lead to this state so that I can adjust my behavior.

When I review my progress, I want to connect creativity, discipline, mood, and long-term direction.

### Needs
- emotional journaling
- creative memory
- project tracking
- pattern discovery
- guided reflection
- meaning-oriented reports
- low-pressure planning

### Shadow value
Shadow acts as a mirror for the creator’s emotional, creative, and behavioral patterns.

---

## 3. Secondary Persona: Life Transition User

### Profile
The Life Transition User is someone going through a major shift:
- new country
- new career
- job search
- breakup
- lifestyle reset
- new identity phase
- health or routine rebuilding
- financial instability
- creative/professional reinvention

### Core problem
Their previous systems no longer work, but the new structure has not formed yet.

They need clarity during instability.

### Typical thoughts
- “Everything is changing.”
- “I don’t know what to prioritize.”
- “I need to rebuild my life structure.”
- “I want to understand what is actually happening with me.”
- “I need something that helps me think through this transition.”

### Jobs To Be Done
When my life feels unstable, I want to track my thoughts, decisions, emotions, and practical tasks so that I can see the bigger picture.

When I make important decisions, I want Shadow to remember the context so that I can revisit it later.

When I feel lost, I want guided questions and reports that help me regain orientation.

### Needs
- decision memory
- emotional tracking
- goal clarity
- life area balance
- weekly review
- cognitive load reduction
- long-term direction tracking

### Shadow value
Shadow becomes a stabilizing structure during transition.

---

## 4. Anti-Persona: Casual Todo User

### Profile
A user who only wants a simple checklist or reminders.

### Why Shadow is not ideal
Shadow may feel too reflective, analytical, or deep for someone who only wants basic task management.

### Better tools for them
- Apple Reminders
- Todoist
- Google Tasks
- simple notes app

---

## 5. Early Adopter Definition

The first ideal user is someone who:
- already feels overloaded
- is willing to reflect daily
- wants to understand themselves better
- has several life areas in motion
- is comfortable with AI
- wants both structure and flexibility
- dislikes manual organization
- is willing to test imperfect MVPs

For the MVP, the founder is the first user.

The product should be built for real personal dogfooding before being generalized.
