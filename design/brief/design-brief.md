# Shadow — Design Brief

## Mood
Dark, warm, personal, reflective, premium, intelligent, calm, slightly mystical, clean.

## Refs
AI dashboards, premium productivity, personal analytics, dark editorial, soft glowing cards, subtle gradients.

## Avoid
Corporate SaaS, Notion clone, gamification, neon cyberpunk, medical tracker, generic task app.

## Palette (draft tokens — refine in `design/tokens/tokens.json`)
- bg-base: #0B0B10
- bg-elev1: #14141B
- bg-elev2: #1C1C26
- text-primary: #F2EEE6
- text-muted: #9892A1
- accent-warm: #C9A36A      (gold-amber)
- accent-cool: #6D7BFF      (twilight indigo)
- danger: #E36161
- success: #6FBF8A

## Typography
- Display: Fraunces or Instrument Serif
- UI: Inter or Geist
- Mono: JetBrains Mono

## Components needed
sidebar, top bar, AI input box, life wheel, metric card, report card, question card, timeline item, tag, mood/energy slider, insight card, task card.

## Screens
Onboarding, Dashboard, AI Inbox, Daily Questions, Life Wheel detail, Reports, Goals, Tasks, Memory/Timeline, Settings.

## Layout philosophy
- Air > density.
- One primary action per screen.
- Cards as units; soft shadow + 1px subtle border.
- Life wheel as signature visual (radial chart, 12 wedges, glow on hover).
